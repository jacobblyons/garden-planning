Field Plan · No. 01 · Spring–Summer 2026

A vegetable garden for a long, bright Bothell summer.

Three beds, three jobs — a classic companion plan for zone 8b. The 4×10 is the sun-lover block. The 3×9 runs along the fence with a trellis for pole beans and cucumbers. The 3×3 sits near the kitchen for herbs. Tomatoes live in three 15-gallon pots between the beds so their disease cycle stays separate.
